# Spiced Cider Brussel Sprouts

---

## Ingredients

* ½ cup garlic oil
* 1 shallot, minced
* 2 tablespoons ginger, minced
* 1 tablespoon cumin
* 1 ½ teaspoon turmeric
* 1 teaspoon cinnamon
* 1 teaspoon fennel seeds
* ½ teaspoon chili paste
* 1 pound brussel sprouts, trimmed and quartered
* 3 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
* 3 tablespoons broth
* 2 tablespoons honey
* ½ cup torn parsley
* 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
* Salt and pepper

---

## Steps

1.  In a skillet, heat the oil over medium-high heat. Add the shallot, ginger, cumin, turmeric, cinnamon, fennel seeds, and chili paste and cook, stirring frequently, until the shallots are translucent, 2 to 3 minutes.
2. Add the brussel sprouts, toss well to combine, and cook until they are fork tender, about 3 minutes.
3. Stir in the vinegar, broth, honey, parsley, and sesame seeds and cook for 2 minutes to heat through. Season to taste.
