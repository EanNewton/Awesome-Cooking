# Consommé à la Brunoise

---

## Ingredients

* 2 ounce carrot
* 2 ounce turnip
* 2 ounce white leek
* 2 ounce celery
* 1 ounce onion
* 5 cup small green peas
* 5 cup green beans
* Chervil for garnish
* 1 gallon consomme


---

## Steps

1.  Heat consomme in a large sauce pan.
2.  Blanch and shock vegetables separately.
3.  Add vegetables and ladle hot consomme, garnish with chervil and serve.
